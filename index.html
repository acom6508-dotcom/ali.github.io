Grammar Basics

Parts of Speech

  • Nouns: Names of people, places, or things (dog, London, happiness)
  • Verbs: Action or state words (run, think, is)
  • Adjectives: Words that describe nouns (beautiful, quick, happy)
  • Adverbs: Words that describe verbs (quickly, very, carefully)

English Tenses

Present Simple

Use: For habits, facts, and general truths

Example: I study English every day.

Present Continuous

Use: For actions happening now

Example: I am studying English right now.
